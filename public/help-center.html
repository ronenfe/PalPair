FlashLive Help Center

FlashLive - Random Video Chat allows users to report child safety concerns in-app. To learn more about reporting requirements, visit the Help center.

My app complies with all relevant child safety laws, and reports to regional and national authorities. To learn more about reporting to the relevant authorities, visit the Help center.

How to report in-app

  • During a chat, tap Report Concern.
  • Describe what happened with as much detail as possible.
  • Submit your report. Our system records it for moderation review.

What to report

  • Child sexual exploitation or endangerment
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Nudity or explicit content shown on camera
  • Threats, coercion, or grooming behavior
  • Any behavior that appears unsafe for children

Note: FlashLive also uses automated AI-based nudity detection. Sessions where explicit content is detected on camera are ended automatically and the account is banned. You do not need to report these — the system handles them instantly.

Emergency situations

If someone is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services right away.

Legal Compliance and Reporting to Authorities

FlashLive complies with applicable child safety laws. When legally required, reports are made to relevant regional and national authorities.

Safety standards URL (CSAE)

Our externally published standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) are available at /safety-standards.html.
