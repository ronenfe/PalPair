Palpair Child Safety Standards (CSAE)

Last updated: 2026-02-20

Zero-tolerance policy

Palpair has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, or account associated with CSAE is strictly prohibited.

Prohibited content and behavior

  • Any sexual content involving minors.
  • Grooming, solicitation, coercion, or exploitation of minors.
  • Sharing, requesting, promoting, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Any attempt to facilitate abuse, trafficking, or endangerment of minors.

In-app reporting

Users can report safety concerns directly in-app using the Report Concern button during chat sessions. Reports are logged for moderation review.

Enforcement

  • Immediate account/session action for suspected CSAE behavior.
  • Preservation of relevant report and technical records as required by law.
  • Cooperation with lawful requests from appropriate authorities.

Legal compliance and authority reporting

Palpair complies with relevant child safety laws and reports to regional and national authorities when required by applicable law.

Contact

For urgent child safety matters related to Palpair, contact: safety@palpair.lol
